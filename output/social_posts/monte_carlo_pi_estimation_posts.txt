Monte Carlo estimation of pi - one of the most elegant algorithms in computational math.

Generate random (x,y) points in [-1,1] x [-1,1]. Count how many satisfy x^2 + y^2 <= 1.

pi = 4 x (inside count) / (total points)

Convergence rate: O(N^(-1/2))

Simple, beautiful, powerful.
