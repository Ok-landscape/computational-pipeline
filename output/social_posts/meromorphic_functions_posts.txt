## What are Meromorphic Functions?

A meromorphic function is a complex function f(z) that's analytic (smooth) everywhere except at a discrete set of isolated points called **poles**, where the function blows up to infinity.

The name comes from Greek: "meros" (part) + "morphe" (form) = "partially holomorphic"

**1. The Gamma Function Î“(z)**
